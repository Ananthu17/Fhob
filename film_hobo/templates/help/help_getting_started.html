Getting started
Indie and Pro Members
Complete your profile:
  1. Please post your professional experiences and achievements in your Bio; you can link your IMDB account, and/or share a link to your website, but to get offcial credits on your FilmHobo page you need to participate in a FilmHobo related project.(Credits and Rating)
  2. Upload your pictures.
  3. Use Settings to set up your preferences for communication, tracking, contact policies, rating visibility and billing.
  4. Set up your interests such as: roles and/or projects you are looking for. When a project or a role with your interest(s) gets posted on the FilmHobo platform you will get a notification so you can apply for cast or crew.
Company Members
Complete Company profile:
  1. Please post Company’s professional experiences and achievements in the Bio; you can link your IMDB account, and/or share a link to the Company’s website.
  2. Attach Company’s employees.
  3. Attach Company’s clients.
  4. Upload pictures.
  5. Use Settings to set up Company’s preferences for communication, tracking, contact policies and billing.
  6. Set up Company’s interests.
Projects
Existing Videos: If you have made a film outside of FilmHobo, you may post this film or a scene from your film on the FilmHobo platform by creating a new project related to this film or scene. You will need to fill out the project’s form, and then attach every actor and all members of your team so they will get the related credits. To get ratings for each team-member and ratings for the whole film, you will need to post the video onto the Screening Room, where premium members can see it, rate it, and give suggestions or feedback.
New Projects:To start a new project you must own a script or have rights to the script. Then, you must fill out the project’s form, creating cast and team roles description, set up sides and passwords, and then post that project onto the project page for FilmHobo members to find your project and apply for available positions. If you have members already committed to your project you can attach them manually
Membership
If you have an Indie membership and you want to start a Feature film or Pilot project, you have two options: (1) Become a Pro member by building up your rating over time, or by joining any of the appropriate Guilds. (2) Get a Production Company membership. You can have two memberships, personal and company (Membership).
Your Network/Friends
Your Network/Friends are important for your success. When you post a project or get attached to a project, you want people to see and rate it. You will rely on your peers in your network to support you.
Activity
This site is for collaborating, learning, inspiring and advancing in the film-making industry. Watch and rate other peers and give them constructive and positive feedback if you wish to receive the same.

Be active if you want to succeed.

How do I find Gigs?
There are three ways to find a project: (1) Set up your interests and when a certain project is posted, you will get a notification so you can apply. (2) Go to the Projects page and search for projects posted near your location. (3) Create a project, attach yourself and let other professionals join you.
Screening Room
This is where the produced videos of all projects are posted, and where the premium members can rate, critique and give suggestions to their peers involved in those projects. Nonmembers have no access to this page, it’s only for internal use.

(Producers don’t have to post their videos for ratings. They can use the platform just to manage their projects.)

Project Page
It is where writers, producers, or companies create and post their projects (script, cast and team, location, genre, format, etc.) for members to apply and audition. On this page, members can search projects and see their details if they click on the project’s box. Members can also rate projects to help other members find the most popular ones.
