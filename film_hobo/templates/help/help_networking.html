Networking
Why do I need my network?
After you have created or participated in a project, you want people to see and rate your work. Your professional network is the first bulk of eyes who can do that. Those people can give you feedback, rate your work, offer you a job, and be instrumental in your career.
How do I build my network?
Watch and Rate videos of other members and leave constructive feedback on their work.
Look for the same minded people you want to work with and send them a friend request.
Track other members who inspire you. They may track you back or send you a friend request.
Invite your friends and/or your professional network to join FilmHobo so they can collaborate and support you.
Help others by participating in their projects, they will do the same for you.
