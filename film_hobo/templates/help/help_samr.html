SAMR
What is SAMR?
It is an abbreviation for Status And Minimum Rating. Where the Status is your membership: Indie, Pro or Company; and the Minimum Rating is the rating you receive for your work as an Actor, Director, Producer, Make-up Artist, etc.
What videos get into the Showcase?Is SAMR important?
Definitely! Companies and Project Creators may use SAMR to limit who can contact them, or who can apply for a job on a project.
For example:
(a) A Production Company or an Agency may set a SAMR submission requirement as “Pro with 4 stars or above”. That means that only Members with that SAMR or above can submit their inquiry to the Company.
(b) a Project Creator may set a SAMR for Talent and Crew to apply for a job. That means that members with a SAMR below that requirement will not be able to apply for the job.
How can I improve my SAMR?
Be active! Use Scene and Short Film projects to build up your SAMR quickly. Become a Pro or have a Company membership and work your rating up.
