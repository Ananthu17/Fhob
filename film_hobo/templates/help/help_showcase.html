Showcase
What is Showcase?
Showcase is the FilmHobo festival for Scenes and Short films.
What videos get into the Showcase?
Any Scene or Short film posted on the FilmHobo platform and is rated 4 stars or above, will advance into the Showcase.
Why do only videos with 4 stars and above advance into Showcase?
We want to make sure that only top-quality videos see the light of the Showcase.
Who can watch Showcase videos?
Hobo Members (free accounts) and the rest of the FilmHobo community.
What is the Top Ten Showcase videos of the month?
The most popular (liked) ten videos will advance into the Top Ten.
Can my video hold the #1 spot in Top Ten for multiple months?
Yes, if it has more “likes” than other videos. Think about it as the Billboard top 40 for music.
My video got into Showcase; can I brag about it?
Definitely.
